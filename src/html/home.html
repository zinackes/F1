THE DRIVERS

Formula 1 drivers, the epitome of racing excellence, ascend through tiers like F4, F3, and F2 before reaching the elite F1 grid. These 20 drivers hold the coveted Super Licence, a racing equivalent of a driver's license. This exclusive credential demands a track record of success in lower categories, ensuring that only the most skilled and accomplished drivers compete at the pinnacle of motorsport.
